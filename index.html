Notepad App Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Notepad by JoshTechApps allows you to request deletion of your account and associated data. This includes notes backed up to Google Drive. Follow the steps below to submit a deletion request.

How to Request Data Deletion

  • Submit a Request: Email us at contactjoshtech@gmail.com with the subject "Notepad App Data Deletion Request." Include the Google account email used for backups in the Notepad app (package: com.joshtechapps.notepad).
  • Data Deleted: We will delete all notes stored in your Google Drive account’s appDataFolder. Deletion will be completed within 7 days of receiving your request.
  • Data Retained: Local data, including notes, passwords, security questions, and preferences, remains on your device until you uninstall the app or manually delete notes via the app’s trash feature.
  • Retention Period: Cloud backup data is not retained after processing your deletion request. Local data persists until app uninstallation.
  • Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity to ensure the security of your request.

Note: Uninstalling the app will delete all local data (notes, passwords, preferences) but will not affect Google Drive backups unless a deletion request is submitted.
